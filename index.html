About Monty

adopted him at the beginning of 2020. He loves all
sorts of food and being outside frolicking his little heart
out. Recently, he has learned how to pull the trashcan
over and has developed a taste for garbage.

  • His greatest love is cheese.
  • He enjoys adventure, but in small doses.
  • He rolls in the dirt far too often for a little, white dog.
